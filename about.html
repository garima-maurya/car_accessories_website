About AutoGear Pro

Built By Drivers, For Drivers

We started as two people frustrated with low-quality car accessories. Today we're a catalog thousands of drivers trust — and the standards haven't changed.

Our Story

Started in a Garage. Still Run Like One.

AutoGear Pro began in 2019 when our founders couldn't find seat covers that actually fit or a car cover that survived one monsoon. So they started sourcing accessories themselves — testing everything on their own cars before it ever reached a customer.

That habit never went away. Every product in our catalog is still chosen the same way: does it hold up, does it fit properly, and would we put it on our own car? If the answer isn't yes to all three, it doesn't make the cut.
